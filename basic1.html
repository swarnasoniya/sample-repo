Reporting Frameworks

The GRI Standards are regularly reviewed to ensure they reflect global best practices for sustainability reporting, helping organizations respond to emerging information demands.

SASB Standards enable businesses to manage and communicate financially-material sustainability topics on a company's financial condition or operating performance.

Visit SASB

CDP is a global disclosure system that helps companies, cities, and regions manage environmental impacts by focusing on energy consumption and water data.

Visit CDP

The TCFD recommendations promote informed decisions through better reporting of climate-related risks and opportunities across governance, strategy, and risk management.

Visit TCFD

SASB Standards enable businesses to manage and communicate financially-material sustainability topics on a company's financial condition or operating performance.

BRSR emphasizes measurable metrics for improved comparability, requiring the top 1,000 listed companies to submit Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reports.

Why choose for your business

Efficient, Transparent ESG Reporting

Footprint simplifies ESG reporting, ensuring reliable data through blockchain technology. This boosts transparency, enabling businesses to meet reporting standards like GRI, SASB, TCFD, and region-specific frameworks.

Comprehensive Supply Chain Sustainability Insights

The tool offers supply chain management reporting, allowing companies to track and report on suppliers' ESG performance. This feature supports responsible sourcing and sustainable supply chain practices.

Data Security and Compliance Assurance

Footprint provides AI and blockchain-enabled security, safeguarding against breaches and fraud. It aligns with reporting standards like BRSR, NASDAQ, and integrates seamlessly through API methods for streamlined data transmission.
